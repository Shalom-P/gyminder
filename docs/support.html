Support

Gyminder is a single-user gym coach that runs entirely on your device. Need help? Email rahulnalluru007@gmail.com and you'll get a reply.

Common questions

Does Gyminder need an account or internet?
No. There's no sign-up and no network connection. Everything works offline and stays on your device.
Where is my workout data stored?
Only on your device. It is never uploaded anywhere. See the Privacy Policy for details.
How do I reset my profile or clear my data?
Use “redo setup” inside the app to reset your profile. Deleting the app removes all of its data permanently.
How do I report a problem or request a feature?
Email rahulnalluru007@gmail.com with your device model and iOS version, and a short description of what happened.
